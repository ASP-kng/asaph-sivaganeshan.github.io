About Me

Get to know more about my journey, skills, and experience

Hello! I'm Asaph

I'm a passionate Web Developer and UI/UX Designer based in Dehiwala-Mount Lavinia, Sri Lanka. With over 8 years of experience in the technology industry, I've had the privilege of working with diverse teams and projects that have shaped my expertise.

My journey began at S. Thomas' College, and I furthered my education at SLIIT, specializing in Software Engineering. Throughout my career, I've evolved from a Digital Marketing Executive to a Senior Executive Web & MIS, gaining invaluable experience in web development, database management, and user experience design.

I'm particularly passionate about creating seamless digital experiences that not only look great but also function flawlessly. My approach combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving to deliver solutions that exceed expectations.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to community projects, or enjoying the beautiful landscapes of Sri Lanka.
